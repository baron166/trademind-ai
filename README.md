# 🚀 TradeMind AI

**AI-powered platform that analyzes global deals and estimates profit for trading electronics, cars, cameras, and watches.**

---

## 🌍 About the Project

TradeMind AI is a smart decision-making tool for traders and resellers.  
It helps users understand whether a deal is profitable before they buy or sell.

Instead of searching multiple websites, users simply enter a product, and the system estimates:

- 💰 Profit potential  
- 🚚 Shipping costs  
- 🧾 Taxes and fees  
- ⚠️ Risk level  
- 📊 Deal rating (Good / Medium / Bad)

---
